Zoho expense login problems often come from account invitations, browser sessions, or mismatched organization access, so confirm the user is entering the correct workspace. Missing receipts usually require employees to resync the zoho expense app or check upload permissions. Delayed reimbursements should be traced through approval history, policy exceptions, and payment processing steps.
